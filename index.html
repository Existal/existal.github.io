Experience

I have always been intrigued by computers. My first experience with programming is using MadCommands plugin for PocketMine (Minecraft PE) servers that makes you able to override existing commands or define your own commands using a simple BASIC-like programming language. I bought my first computer, and I learned how to develop my own Minecraft PE plugins with the PocketMine-MP (PMMP) API. Afterward, I experimented with different programming languages including Python, Java, and JavaScript (Node.js). I interned at a computer repair store in 2019, and I learned about customer service, computer components, and repairing computers. They believed that helping people find the help and information when and where they need it is going to make a brighter world. I am currently expanding my knowledge on website and game development.


I always bring maximum effort, focus, and commitment to everything I do. It's an encouragement to do my best with no excuses. I always try to perform at the highest level that is possible for myself. For better or for worse, the majority of our lives will be spent at work. Do not spend that time unhappy. It is essential to wake up happy and elevated to go to work instead of dreading the new week. There is no shortage of people who dread getting up in the morning. People who kill time and watch the clock. People who complain nonstop about their jobs. People who "invest" in lottery tickets in the hope of never, ever having to work again. People who have no energy at the end of the day and just can't figure out where it all went wrong. I realized that it's essential to find enjoyment in your career because you will be working for forty to fifty years of your life. I love programming computers; therefore, I hope to obtain a career as a Computer Programmer.


However, you have a choice when it comes to your job. I found out that you could flip the switch and bring your A-Game to work every day and in every way, or you can choose to give nothing more than a mediocre, half-hearted effort and simply try to avoid getting fired. I tyr to bring the same passion to the workplace that I do to my recreational pursuits. I leanred that work experience will be the most important factor in determining the work you will do for the rest of your life. Every job is as critically important in defining and preparing you for your next job. I try to set myself apart from the crowd and propel myself to the top. I learned that if I plan to make it big someday, I cannot afford to do anything less than my absolute best.
